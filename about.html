What HCG means to us.

Why make games?

Our Goal

Our primary hobby is something we have always loved; games and game development.
We want to bring back the feeling that games had in the early days,
Games that are innovative, made with intention, and do something different.

JB

Making cool experiences that people will remember is a true passion of mine.
As long as I can remember the dream that is making games has been a common aspiration throughout my entire life.
Whether its the magic I felt from playing card games at card shops or playing final fantasy 6 on Saturday morning with my dad and brothers,
gaming has created some of the greatest memories and I hope to bring that feeling to others. I love cool games!

Komical

I have always been interested in games, ive been playing them my whole life.
Making games though, I always thought was a daunting task that only smart people could do.
Jb gave me some inspiration to get into blender and 3D art back in 2021 and later Godot and I havent Looked back since.
